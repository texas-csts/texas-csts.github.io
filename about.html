Welcome to Texas CSTS

(CSTS) supports Computer Science transfer students by providing a community for new and existing transfer students and giving aid to prospective transfer student.

What we do

Essay Workshops

In the spring semester, we host many essay workshops to help you revise and brainstorm ideas for your trasnfer application essay.

Resume Workshops

We host resume workshops to give you sugestions to make to your resume and make it competitive

Socials!

We will host many social! Such as game night, pizza night and a coding night

Why Join CSTS?

  • Make life long friends with fellow students!
  • Get personal connections to transfer students
  • Be more knowledged with the transfer process
  • Make yourself a more competitive applicant

    • Who Can Join

  • Any CS Transfer students
  • Internal, External, and Accelerated (ATP) transfer
  • Must be a current UT student
  • No dues (except donations for org swag)
